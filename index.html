Hello, I'm Ruth

I am an emerging artist who taught myself to paint, discovering my passion during the pandemic. What started as a way to pass the time quickly became a deep creative journey. Working primarily with oil paints, I enjoy exploring a wide range of subjects, from animals and landscapes to portraits, always driven by intuition and experimentation.

If you’re looking to buy an original painting or commission a custom artwork, feel free to reach out via Instagram or email. I love bringing unique, hand-painted pieces to life and would be happy to create something just for you.
