The Side of Thought

This portrait shows a woman in profile against a bright green background. Her dark brown hair and soft skin tones are rendered with smooth gradients and precision. The unfinished brushstrokes on her hair add a contemporary artistic edge, suggesting thought, memory, or transformation.

Original oil Painting
Format: 40 x 40 cm
Material: High-quality, double-depth canvas (gallery wrap), hand-primed with MALZEIT gesso
